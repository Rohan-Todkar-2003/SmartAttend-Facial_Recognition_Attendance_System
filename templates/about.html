About Us

Welcome to SmartAttend, where innovation meets efficiency. We are proud to introduce our cutting-edge Face Recognition Attendance System, designed to revolutionize the way organizations manage employee attendance. Our system leverages advanced facial recognition technology to provide a seamless, secure, and accurate attendance tracking solution.

Our mission is to simplify attendance management while enhancing security and productivity. We aim to provide organizations with a reliable, user-friendly, and feature-rich system that eliminates the inefficiencies of traditional attendance methods.

What We Offer

Our Face Recognition Attendance System is a state-of-the-art solution that combines artificial intelligence, machine learning, and robust security features to deliver unparalleled performance. Here’s what sets us apart:

Multi-Format Sampling

Accepts .jpg files for sampling, in addition to live photo capture, making it flexible and adaptable to your needs.

Automated Email Notifications

Employees receive instant email notifications when their attendance is marked, ensuring transparency and real-time updates.

Comprehensive Attendance Analytics

View detailed statistics on daily, weekly, and monthly attendance percentages, empowering you to make data-driven decisions.

Spoof Detection

Our advanced anti-spoofing technology ensures that only genuine faces are recognized, preventing fraudulent attempts.

Downloadable Reports

Easily download attendance sheets for the day or the entire duration, simplifying record-keeping and compliance.

General Information Management

Add and manage employee details, roles, and other relevant information within the system.

Floating ChatBot

Offers structured responses to FAQs on attendance, policies, and system features.
